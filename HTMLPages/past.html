Past Event Reviews

Here we highlight what actually happened: when artists came on, how the crowd felt, and what people would do differently next time.

Post Malone – Twelve Carat Tour

Past Event Review

Venue: Madison Square Garden (New York, NY)

Rating: 4.5/5

Doors opened around 6:30 PM and the opener finished by 8:00 PM. Post came on close to 8:30 PM, which most people felt was reasonable. Security lines outside were long but moved steadily. Inside, the sound was loud but very clear, and the crowd stayed engaged the whole time. Many reviews mentioned that the most emotional songs were near the end, so leaving early would mean missing some of the best moments.

Lil Uzi Vert – Neon Nights Live

Past Event Review

Venue: SoFi Stadium (Inglewood, CA)

Rating: 4/5

The opener wrapped up around 9:00 PM, but Uzi didn’t come on until about 9:45 PM. Some fans loved the build-up, while others felt the wait was a bit long. Security inside the pit areas was active, breaking up rougher mosh sections when needed. People described the show as intense but fun, with heavy bass and strong visuals. Several reviewers suggested standing farther back if you don’t want to be in the middle of the crowd pushing forward.

City Rooftop Sessions

Past Event Review

Venue: Skyline Rooftop (Brooklyn, NY)

Rating: 4/5

This event had a relaxed start, with the first act going on around 7:00 PM and the main artist starting closer to 9:00 PM. The view of the city skyline was the main highlight, and the crowd was described as friendly and low-key. Drinks and small bites were available but a bit pricey. Most attendees said they would go again, but they would bring a light jacket since the rooftop got windy and cooler later in the evening.

Summer Kickoff Jam

Past Event Review

Venue: Liberty Park Stage (Newark, NJ)

Rating: 3.5/5

This festival featured several artists across the afternoon and evening. The main headliner came on a little later than expected, around 10:00 PM, after some technical delays. Fans liked the variety of performers but mentioned that food and restroom lines became very long after sunset. Many reviews said the atmosphere was fun and energetic, but recommended arriving early and planning breaks so you don’t miss the acts you care about most.

Acoustic Evenings Live

Past Event Review

Venue: Downtown Pavilion (New York, NY)

Rating: 4.5/5

This show had seated sections and a more relaxed timeline. The main artist came out around 8:15 PM after a single opener. Reviews highlighted the clear sound, respectful crowd, and short lines for entry and concessions. People who attended said it was a great option for someone’s first concert because the environment felt calm, organized, and not overwhelming.
